Concrete Surfing is an "aprecciation post website" born in Madrid in 2023, with the intention to provide a channel in which to share info and material about the Surfskate movement, within a local focus, in Spain. The goal is to gather pro and amateur content so we can share it with the rest of the skating community.

Concrete Surfing es un "blog de apreciación" que nace en Madrid en el 2023, con la intención de proponer un canal por el cual poder compartir información y material acerca del movimiento Surfskate, con un efoque local, en España. La meta es recolectar contenido amateur y pro, para poder compartirlo con el resto de la comunidad Skate.

We present to you information regarding national talent, upcoming events, surfcamps, boards, city spots, and much more. We encourage you to send your material, as we'll be more than happy to show it!
